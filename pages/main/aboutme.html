About Me

I am a twenty-two year old graduate of Algonquin College's Game Development program living in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada.

I've enjoyed playing around with computers for as long as I can remember—the first computer I used was the family HP Pavilion 4445 running Windows 98 and later XP.

I started making "games" when I discovered you Action Buttons in PowerPoint 2003, allowing for interactive slideshows. Since then, I've played with any kind of interactive media I could get my hands on. I learned Python in 2014 developing text-based adventure games based on The Transformers and started my journey with Unity and C# in 2015. I enjoy throwing myself at new challenges and teaching myself to use new programs, languages, operating systems and interfaces.

My favourite games are SimCity 4, Zoo Tycoon 2, Microsoft Flight Simulator 2004, RollerCoaster Tycoon 3, MVP Baseball 2005, ATV: Offroad Fury 2, Gran Turismo 3, Automobilista 2, Assetto Corsa and Kerbal Space Program

When I'm not programming or working on my personal site, I enjoy reading, photography, cycling and ice skating amongst other hobbies. I enjoy being outside, moving my body and enjoying nature no matter the weather.

I'm always listening to music—I love Classic Rock, Progressive Rock, Grunge and their respective offshoots. My favourite bands are Pink Floyd, Green Day, Genesis, Dire Straits, Led Zeppelin, R.E.M., The Clash, Nine Inch Nails and The Smashing Pumpkins.

My favourite movie is The Terminator (1984) and my favourite book is A Farewell To Arms by Ernest Hemingway.
